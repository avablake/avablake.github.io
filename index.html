Free Books!

When you join the Advanced Reader Team you recieve a copy of my books before they're available on Amazon, absolutely free of charge. You also have the opportunity to email me back and tell me what you thought of the book, and point out anything I might have messed up, so that the final product that gets sent to Amazon is the best possible book.

Sign Up

Reviews Encouraged, Not Required

I really encourage (but don't require) my Advanced Reader Team to take a couple minutes and leave a review on Amazon. We indie writers are competing with all the big publishing companies on Amazon. By leaving an honest review you're helping to support independent writers like me, who are working hard to make a career for themselves. It doesn't have to be a 400 word essay, just whatever you're comfortable with.

I'm Ava Blake, author of romance fiction. I really fell in love with this genre when I was sick, laid up in the hospital. Reading romance books on my Kindle was a great distraction that got me through some hard times, and in the interest of paying it forward I sincerely hope that my writing can be of some benefit and enjoyment to you, dear reader. So grab a book, sign up for the newsletter and please let me know what you think!
